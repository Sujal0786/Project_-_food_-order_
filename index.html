    Frequently asked questions

    What are the login hours?

    I see you're looking for our login hours! I can tell you that our login hours are from 5:00 pm to 10:00 pm. Please note that these hours may be subject to change, so it's always a good idea to check our website or call us to confirm..

    What do i get my refund?

    To request a refund, speak with a manager and explain the problem with your meal, whether it's related to food quality, service, or something else. Work with the manager to find a resolution that you're comfortable with, which might be a refund, a complimentary dish, or something else. Remember to stay calm and polite when requesting a refund, and please be honest and respectful as refund abuse and chargeback fraud are serious issues for restaurants.

    How long it will take food to arrive?

    The time it takes for food to arrive from a restaurant can vary depending on several factors, such as the type of restaurant, the distance from the restaurant to your location, and the method of delivery or pickup. On average, fast-food diners expect their orders to be ready within 5 minutes or less, while customers at casual or fine-dining restaurants may expect a wait time of 20 minutes or more.

    Does your restaurant has both veg and non veg?

    Yes, our restaurant offers both vegetarian and non-vegetarian food options.

    What is cost of food delivery?

    The cost of food delivery from our restaurant varies depending on the delivery method and location. On average, the delivery fee can range from $1.99 to $7.99, depending on the distance from the restaurant to your location. Some popular food delivery apps, such as Grubhub, Uber Eats, and DoorDash, may also charge additional service fees, taxes, and tips.

    Who is eligible for pro membership?

    You must have placed a minimum of 10 orders within the last 3 months. You must have a minimum total spend of $200 within the last 3 months. You must be a registered user on our website or mobile app. If you meet these criteria, you will be automatically enrolled in our Pro Membership program and start enjoying benefits such as free delivery, exclusive discounts, priority customer support, early access to new menu items, and bonus rewards points.
